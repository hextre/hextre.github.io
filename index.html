About

I am Mr. Yangyadatta Tripathy, Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee. My research focuses on power systems, microgrids, renewable integration, optimization, and advanced power electronics.

Education

  • Ph.D. (2023), IIT Roorkee
  • M.Tech (2014), IIT Roorkee
  • B.Tech (2012), Silicon Institute of Technology, Bhubaneswar
  • Intermediate (2008), Binayak Acharya College
  • Matriculate (2006), Medical Campus Govt. High School

Experience

Assistant Professor, DIT University (2014–2017). Taught FACTS Devices, Electromagnetic Field Theory, conducted laboratory sessions, and evaluated student performance.

Internships

  • Summer Trainee, Central Tool Room & Training Center, Bhubaneswar (2010) — Siemens PLC, Ladder Logic Programming
  • Summer Trainee, Silicon Institute of Technology (2011) — Simulation in PSIM and PSCAD

Projects

  • Hybrid Renewable Microgrid for Agriculture & Dairy (2019–2022): AC/DC smart microgrid with solar-agriculture integration and techno-economic analysis.
  • Wavelet Transform + ELM for Fault Detection (2013–2014): Novel approach for detecting and classifying power transmission line faults.
  • Transient Over-voltage and Surge Protection (2012): Insulation coordination in substations.
  • ZCS Buck Converter for DC Motor (2012): Soft-switching based motor drive implementation.
