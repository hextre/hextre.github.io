About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee . My group works at the intersection of power systems, optimization, and power electronics. We develop algorithms and hardware-in-the-loop experiments for resilient, low-carbon grids.
